Section 3: A Challenge With My Media Tools

One challenge I often run into is tool fragmentation across devices and platforms. For example, I might start reading something on my phone, take notes on a laptop, and later try to find the same reference while working on a different machine.

This creates problem for me:

  • Context switching cost: even small interruptions (notifications, switching apps) can break focus, especially during deep work.

Potential Solutions: I am trying to establish a single "source of truth" for notes (centralizing links + summaries). Additionally, I now utilize system-level Focus Modes on my phone and laptop. By toggling a dedicated "Work Mode," I can automatically filter out non-essential notifications to protect my attention.
